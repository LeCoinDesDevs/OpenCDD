Small utility to parse time strings.

Format

duration    ::= integer unit | time
integer     ::= digit+
digit       ::= "0"..."9"
unit        ::= "sec" | "min" | "hr" | "jr" | "sem" | "mo" | "an" 
time        ::= digit{1,2} ":" digit{2} ":" digit{2}

Examples :

  • 4 days: 4jr
  • 3 weeks: 3sem
  • 10 hours: 10h
  • 2 hours et 13 minutes: 2:13:00
